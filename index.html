Finding Local Organic Food Based on Trust

We are a marketplace for local organic farmers to connect with their customer base via a responsive web app. Foode Dapp is an e-commerce site built with the power of the blockchain. The real power of the blockchain is Foode using the blockchain to allow farmers the chance to show transparency of their supply chain logistics from farm to consumer. Using the crowdsourcing of reputation to build trust that they are organic. Allowing farmers to connect with locals and have a history of digital certificates that prove their trustworthiness.
